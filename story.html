The Story — English

Two Websites.
One Hospital Group.
Worlds Apart.

01

The Sleeping Giant

Hayat National Hospitals — 6 branches, 269 doctors, 1,050 beds across Saudi Arabia. A healthcare powerhouse with a traditional corporate website that showed the what, but not the how.

02

The Transformation

BrainSAIT built an AI-native operational dashboard — a real-time nerve center integrating 10 insurance partners, FHIR R4 standards, and predictive analytics. Same hospital. Completely different digital reality.

03

The Results

98.6% claim approval (vs 82% industry average). SAR 9.8M recovered through AI-driven denial analysis. 57 duplicate claims caught in a single batch. Real money. Real impact.

04

The Voice

بسمة (Basma) — a bilingual AR/EN voice assistant built on ElevenLabs v2.1, available 24/7. Patients interact naturally. The system listens, understands, responds.
