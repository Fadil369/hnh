## 4. VISION, MISSION, VALUES

### Vision
To be a leader in healthcare in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and the Middle East. Through continuous innovation and providing high-quality healthcare meeting the highest global standards. Goal: to be a center of medical excellence.

Strategic commitment: Providing distinguished medical services through horizontal expansion — investing in establishing and operating a network of hospitals and specialized medical units covering most regions of the Kingdom, in accordance with the highest quality standards, maintaining patient safety using the latest medical devices for speedy diagnosis and treatment.

### Mission
Providing distinguished, integrated, patient-focused healthcare. Committed to innovative and comprehensive medical treatment supported by highly experienced specialists. Quality, efficiency, and compassion are the essential components of exceptional healthcare.

### Values
1. Superior and professional healthcare
2. Honesty and credibility
3. Commitment to social responsibility towards society
4. Full commitment to protecting the privacy of visitors and patients
5. Commitment to Islamic teachings
6. Quality and safety

Core values: integrity, perfection, and privacy.

### CEO Message
The mission demands precision in selecting medical and administrative cadres at the highest scientific and technical levels to achieve strategic goals. These plans raise the level of medical services in the private sector based on modern scientific foundations through applying the highest international quality and safety systems, and moving forward with international accreditation programs.

Beyond medical services, the group embraces its national duty and social responsibility — educating and raising community awareness regarding health problems and diseases through lectures, awareness seminars, and field visits to entities, institutions, and companies.
